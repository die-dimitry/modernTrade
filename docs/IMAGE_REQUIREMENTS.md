# Image Requirements for Modern Trade Website

## Testimonial Images

All testimonial images should be placed in: `frontend/public/testimonials/`

### Required Images:

1. **aarav-sharma.jpg**
   - **Description**: Professional headshot of a male software developer
   - **Requirements**: 
     - Circular crop ready (will be displayed as circle)
     - Size: 200x200px minimum (square)
     - Professional business attire
     - Neutral background or office setting
     - Friendly, confident expression
   - **Path**: `frontend/public/testimonials/aarav-sharma.jpg`

2. **priya-kapoor.jpg**
   - **Description**: Professional headshot of a female marketing executive
   - **Requirements**:
     - Circular crop ready (will be displayed as circle)
     - Size: 200x200px minimum (square)
     - Professional business attire
     - Neutral background or office setting
     - Friendly, confident expression
   - **Path**: `frontend/public/testimonials/priya-kapoor.jpg`

3. **rajesh-patel.jpg**
   - **Description**: Professional headshot of a male chartered account
   - **Requirements**:
     - Circular crop ready (will be displayed as circle)
     - Size: 200x200px minimum (square)
     - Professional business attire (preferably formal)
     - Neutral background or office setting
     - Friendly, confident expression
   - **Path**: `frontend/public/testimonials/rajesh-patel.jpg`

### Image Guidelines:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Recommended size: 400x400px (square) for high resolution displays
- Quality: High quality, professional photos
- Background: Neutral, professional (white, gray, or subtle office background)
- Style: Modern, professional business photos
- Note: The website will automatically fallback to generated avatars if images are not found

## Creating the Directory Structure

Create the testimonials folder if it doesn't exist:
```bash
mkdir -p frontend/public/testimonials
```

Then place the three testimonial images in this folder with the exact names specified above.
